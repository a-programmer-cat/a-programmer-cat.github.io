Introduce to mosquito

Mosquitoes are a family of small flies consisting of 3,600 species.



Disadvantages of Mosquitoes 1

The mosquito's saliva is transferred to the host during the bite, and can cause an itchy rash.

Disadvantages of Mosquitoes 2

In addition, blood-feeding species can ingest pathogens while biting, and transmit them to other hosts. Those species include vectors of parasitic diseases such as malaria and filariasis, and arboviral diseases such as yellow fever and dengue fever.
